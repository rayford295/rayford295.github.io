## About

This is the source code for [rayford295.github.io](https://rayford295.github.io), built on the [AcadHomepage](https://github.com/RayeRen/acad-homepage.github.io) template.

**Yifan Yang** is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Geography at Texas A&M University, advised by [Dr. Lei Zou](https://www.geoearlab.com/people) in the [GEAR Lab](https://www.geoearlab.com). His research lies at the intersection of **Responsible GeoAI** and **Autonomous GeoAI**, developing explainable and trustworthy geospatial AI systems for spatial decision-making and disaster resilience.

He is also the founder and maintainer of [AutonomousGeoAI4Science](https://github.com/AutoGeoAI4Sci), an open research initiative dedicated to advancing autonomous and responsible GeoAI.

### Research Interests

- Spatial Data Science · GeoAI · AI4Science
- GIScience · Disaster Resilience
- Responsible & Explainable AI in Geospatial Contexts

### Education

- **Ph.D. in Geography** — Texas A&M University (2024 – Present)
- **M.S. in Spatial Data Science** — University of Southern California
- **B.S. in Software Engineering** — Hainan University

## Run Locally

```bash

bash run_server.sh
# Open http://127.0.0.1:4000
```
